# <i class="fas fa-people-group"></i>Current Opportunities

We are committed to building an inclusive, supportive research community and welcome students from all backgrounds, including those historically underrepresented in engineering and the sciences.

We have open opportunities for students interested in environmental engineering, water and wastewater treatment, environmental chemistry, and electrochemistry. Please reach out to discuss your interests—we look forward to meeting you and learning together!

{% include section.html %}

## Undergraduates

We welcome undergraduate researchers through several University of Washington programs.

- [UW Undergraduate Research Program (URP)](https://www.washington.edu/undergradresearch/): Support and placement for undergraduate research across campus
- [Mary Gates Research Scholarship](https://expd.uw.edu/mge/apply/research/): Competitive scholarships supporting undergraduate research

## Graduate students

We work with students in the [Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering PhD program](https://www.ce.washington.edu/phd). Full-time PhD students are supported through research and teaching assistantships and departmental fellowships—see the [CEE funding page](https://www.ce.washington.edu/phd/funding) for details. We also welcome students from allied programs across the university.

## Postdoctoral researchers

We are happy to support prospective postdoctoral researchers in developing fellowship applications. Please reach out to discuss potential projects and funding.

## Funding

### University of Washington funding

- [UW Graduate School — list of fellowships](https://grad.uw.edu/graduate-student-funding/fellowships/list-of-fellowships/): Central directory of UW and external fellowships
- [CEE department fellowships](https://www.ce.washington.edu/mycee/hr/fellowship): Merit-based departmental fellowships
- [Clean Energy Institute (CEI) Graduate Fellowship](https://www.cei.washington.edu/education/uw-graduate-students/graduate-fellowship/): Fellowships for PhD students advancing clean-energy research
- [College of the Environment funding](https://environment.uw.edu/students/current-students/scholarships-funding/graduate-scholarships-funding/other-graduate-funding-opportunities/): Environment-focused scholarships and fellowships for graduate students

### Environmental engineering & water funding

- [NSF Graduate Research Fellowship Program (GRFP)](https://www.nsfgrfp.org/): Three years of support for research-based graduate study in STEM
- [EPA Research Fellowships for Graduate Students](https://www.epa.gov/research-fellowships/research-fellowships-graduate-students): Fellowships for environmental research
- [NDSEG Fellowship](https://ndseg.org/): Department of Defense fellowship for graduate study in science and engineering
- [Ford Foundation Fellowships](https://sites.nationalacademies.org/pga/fordfellowships/): Fellowships supporting diversity in the academy
- [Hertz Foundation Fellowship](https://www.hertzfoundation.org/): Fellowships in the applied physical, biological, and engineering sciences
- [ARCS Foundation](https://arcsfoundation.org/): Awards for outstanding STEM graduate students
- [AEESP awards & fellowships](https://aeesp.org/): Environmental engineering and science professional society opportunities
- [The Water Research Foundation](https://www.waterrf.org/): Research funding and student opportunities in water

_Deadlines and eligibility change year to year—please confirm details on each program's website before applying._
