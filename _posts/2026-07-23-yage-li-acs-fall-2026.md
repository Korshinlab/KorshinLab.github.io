Congratulations to **Yage Li**, whose abstract has been accepted for a poster presentation at **ACS Fall 2026** (August 23–27, 2026).

**"Landfill gas treatment solids as sinks and sources of arsenic and antimony in renewable biomethane operations: Release of As/Sb under disposal-relevant conditions"**

The poster appears in the session *Characterization and Recovery of Critical Minerals and Materials From Solid and Aqueous Waste Streams*, organized by the Division of Geochemistry (GEOC) and the Division of Environmental Chemistry (ENVR).

**When:** Monday, August 24, 2026, 12:00 – 2:00 PM
**Where:** Hall F2 (Posters), McCormick Place Convention Center

Nice work, Yage!
